Novembersoundsbetter hails from Tampere, Finland, despite their huge sound is nodding towards the americas. Regarding musical genres, these guys wander the road not taken. However, some elements from post-hardcore can be heard as well as some movie-like sonar sceneries from post-rock. NSB are like a fresh breath of air in the Finnish rock/metal scene even though their release catalog already includes 3 albums and several EP:s and the band has been around since 2004. In their live shows you will witness some raw high energy music finessed with sensitive emotional barrage.
