No Place Like Here

Out now

Novembersoundsbetter hails from Tampere, Finland despite their huge sound nodding towards the americas. Regarding musical genres, these guys wander the road not taken. However, some elements from post-hardcore and nu-metal can be heard as well as some movie-like sonar sceneries from post-rock. NSB are like a fresh breath of air in the finnish rock/metal scene even though their release catalog already includes 3 albums and several EP:s. In their live shows you will witness some raw high energy music finessed with sensitive emotional barrage.
